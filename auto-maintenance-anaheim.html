Routine Auto Maintenance & Oil Changes in Anaheim, CA

Preventative care is the best way to avoid costly breakdowns. We offer rapid oil and filter changes, fluid checks, 30k/60k/90k mileage log book services, and general diagnostics at our shop.

Recommended Maintenance Intervals

  • Standard Engine Oil & Filter Change: Every 3,000 to 5,000 miles
  • Synthetic Engine Oil & Filter Change: Every 7,500 to 10,000 miles
  • Cabin and Engine Air Filters: Checked every 12,000 miles
  • Factory Recommended Inspections: At 30,000, 60,000, and 90,000 miles

Our Maintenance Principles

Every oil change at our shop includes a complimentary fluid level top-off, safety visual check of belts and hoses, and checking of tire pressures. We keep complete digital records of your service dates. We only suggest maintenance items that are actually due, and we never pressure you into buying accessories.

Maintenance Services We Offer

  • Conventional and Full Synthetic Oil Changes
  • Engine Coolant and Power Steering Flushes
  • Spark Plugs and Cabin Air Filter Fitting
  • Battery Diagnostic Testing & Replacement
