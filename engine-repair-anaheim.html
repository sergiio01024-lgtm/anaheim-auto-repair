Engine Diagnostics & Repair in Anaheim, CA

Our mechanics use advanced diagnostics to decode engine warning lights and troubleshoot mechanical engine faults. We pinpoint issues accurately so we only replace what is necessary.

Common Warning Signs & Symptoms

  • Check Engine or Service Soon light is on
  • Engine running rough, misfiring, or shaking
  • Overheating or steam coming from the hood
  • Leaks of oil or green/orange coolant under the engine

Our Service & Repair Process

  1. Electronic diagnostic scan to retrieve error codes
  2. Manual verification of sensors, wiring, and vacuum lines
  3. Clear explanation of immediate vs. deferrable repairs
  4. Component repair and resetting of the onboard system monitors

Related Services We Offer

  • Spark Plug & Ignition Coil Kits
  • Timing Belts & Water Pumps
  • Valve Cover Gasket Leak Repair
  • Radiators & Thermostats

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it safe to drive with the Check Engine light on?
If the light is steady, you should schedule a diagnostic scan soon. If the light is flashing, pull over immediately to prevent severe engine damage.
Q: How often should timing belts be replaced?
Usually between 60,000 and 100,000 miles to prevent timing belt breaks that cause severe engine damage.
