Catalytic Converter Replacement in Anaheim, CA

As a Yelp-verified specialty shop in Anaheim, CA, we specialize in emissions diagnostic assessments and catalytic converter replacements that comply with California's strict CARB standards.

When to Replace Your Catalytic Converter

  • Your check engine light is on with codes P0420 or P0430 (Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold)
  • The vehicle failed its smog check due to high emissions or physical catalyst damage
  • You hear a rattling sound from the exhaust when idling or accelerating
  • Your converter has been stolen, requiring a secure replacement and theft protection

The Replacement & Theft Prevention Process

Replacing a converter in California requires strict compliance with CARB regulations. We verify the specific part number required for your engine group, cut out the old unit, weld in the new certified catalytic converter, and reset emissions monitors. We also install custom heavy-duty steel security shields to protect your catalytic converter from future theft.
