Brake Repair & Suspension Service in Anaheim, CA

From routine brake pad replacements to complex shock, strut, and steering suspension diagnostics, our ASE-certified mechanics ensure your vehicle stops and rides safely on Orange County roads.

When to Inspect Your Brakes and Struts

  • Squeaking, squealing, or grinding noises when pressing the brake pedal
  • The vehicle pulls to one side or the steering wheel vibrates when braking
  • Your brake pedal feels unusually soft, spongy, or goes down to the floorboard
  • Excessive bouncing over bumps, body sway during turns, or uneven tire wear

Our Braking & Steering Service Standards

Safety is our top priority. We perform complete digital inspections on brake pads, rotors, drums, lines, and calipers. We only use high-grade ceramic or semi-metallic friction pads. For suspension work, we replace worn shocks, struts, ball joints, control arm bushings, and CV axles to restore factory handling.
