Brakes & Suspension Service in Anaheim, CA

Our brake and suspension services keep your vehicle stopping quickly and riding smoothly. We inspect brake linings, calipers, master cylinders, and replace worn suspension components to restore responsive handling.

Common Warning Signs & Symptoms

  • Squealing or grinding noises when braking
  • Brake pedal feels soft, spongy, or goes to the floor
  • Steering wheel shakes or pulls when braking
  • Vehicle bounces excessively over bumps or sways in turns

Our Service & Repair Process

  1. Multi-point inspection of brake pads, rotors, hoses, and fluid levels
  2. Visual and push check of shock absorbers and steering linkage
  3. Precise component replacement with premium ceramic pads or heavy-duty struts
  4. Final road test to ensure stopping power and handling alignment

  • Shock & Strut Replacement
  • CV Axle & Boot Repair
  • Tie Rods & Ball Joints

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I change my brake pads?
Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles, depending on driving habits and brake material.
Q: What causes a spongy brake pedal?
Usually air in the brake lines, a fluid leak, or a failing master cylinder. This requires immediate diagnostic inspection.
