Transmission Service & Repair in Anaheim, CA

Avoid costly transmission rebuilds with routine fluid maintenance and expert diagnostics. We flush fluid, replace filters, check solenoids, and repair manual clutches.

Common Warning Signs & Symptoms

  • Delayed or slipping gear shifting
  • Grinding or shuddering when shifting
  • Transmission fluid leaking (reddish fluid)
  • Transmission temperature warning light is on

Our Service & Repair Process

  1. Transmission fluid level and quality check
  2. Scanning TCM for gear ratio and solenoid faults
  3. Transmission fluid drain and fill, or filter exchange
  4. Linkage and clutch adjustment or replacement

Related Services We Offer

  • Transmission Fluid Flush
  • Clutch Replacement Kits
  • Differential Fluid Check
  • Transmission Solenoids

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often does transmission fluid need servicing?
Typically every 30,000 to 60,000 miles for automatic transmissions to prevent fluid breakdown and slipping.
Q: What does transmission slipping feel like?
The engine revs up high when you accelerate, but the car hesitates or is slow to shift and gain speed.
