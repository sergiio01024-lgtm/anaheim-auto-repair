Transmission Repair & Service in Anaheim, CA

Keep your car shifting smoothly. We provide manual and automatic transmission inspections, diagnostic checks, fluid changes, and mechanical repairs at our Anaheim location.

Signs of a Slipping or Damaged Transmission

  • Rough or delayed gear shifting or a feeling of "slipping" gears
  • Grinding or shaking sensations when the car shifts gears
  • Whining, clunking, or humming noises from the transmission area
  • Puddling reddish-brown fluid under your car (transmission fluid leak)

Our Transmission Care Approach

Routine fluid maintenance is the key to extending the life of your transmission. We perform automatic transmission fluid drains and refills, filter replacements, and complete fluid flushes. When shifting errors occur, we scan the Transmission Control Module (TCM) for solenoids, sensors, and electronic faults before recommending physical repairs.

Transmission Services We Offer

  • Transmission Solenoid & Sensor Diagnostics
  • Clutch Replacements (Manual Transmissions)
